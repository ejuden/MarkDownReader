Navigating Documents

Table of Contents

The sidebar displays a table of contents generated from the headings in your document. Click any heading to scroll directly to that section.

To show or hide the table of contents:

  • Click the sidebar icon in the toolbar, or
  • Press ⌘⇧T, or
  • Choose View › Toggle Table of Contents.

Searching

To find text within a document:

  1. Press ⌘F or click the magnifying glass in the toolbar.
  2. Type your search term. Matches are highlighted as you type.
  3. Use the up/down arrows in the search bar (or press Enter) to move between matches.
  4. Press Escape to close the search bar and clear highlights.

Zoom

Adjust the document zoom level to your preference:

ActionShortcut
Zoom in⌘+
Zoom out⌘-
Reset to 100%⌘0

You can also use the zoom buttons in the toolbar. The current zoom percentage is displayed between the buttons.

Dark Mode

MarkDown Reader automatically follows your macOS appearance setting. When you switch between Light and Dark mode in System Settings › Appearance, the document view updates immediately.

Links

Clicking a web link in a document opens it in your default browser. Links to other files or anchors within the document are handled internally.

Printing & Exporting

Printing a Document

To print the currently open document:

  1. Click the printer icon in the toolbar, or press ⌘P.
  2. The macOS print dialog will appear.
  3. Choose your printer, page range, number of copies, and other settings.
  4. Click Print.
Tip: In the print dialog, you can choose "Save as PDF" from the PDF dropdown in the lower-left corner as an alternative way to create a PDF.

Exporting as PDF

To save the document as a PDF file:

  1. Click the export icon (down arrow) in the toolbar, or press ⌘⇧E.
  2. Choose a location and filename in the save dialog.
  3. Click Save.

The PDF is generated from the rendered document, preserving all formatting, code blocks, tables, and images.
